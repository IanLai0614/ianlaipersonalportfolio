Product Concept

Structscrew & cork are a system of wine screw and cork, which are designed in structuralism style. And can be used in a human factor engineering way.

Aboat Structuralism

Structuralism is to figure out the relationship between the object. Structuralism is the name that is given to a wide range of discourses that study underlying structures of signification.

Design Concept

Structscrew & cork are a system of wine screw and cork, which are designed in structuralism style. To express the structuralism style on the products , I made the screw and cork formed by a bent way. So you can clearly see the broken line, and different angle of flats. In this way, not only can show the relationship of the flats in the space to show the structuralism style, but also can use it in a human factor engineering way.
