#BOOK-DESIGN

成大工設107畢刊

Concept

The overall cover is the image of the window, meaning " 同窗", which means classmate in chinese, and the main vision in the window is a labyrinth. It represents thousands of choices in life, and we all choose to enter NCKUID. I hope everyone can accomplish one thing together and leave a memorial by the main vision.

成功登大人營手冊

Concept

This manual was designed for freshman camp in 2016. The theme of that year was " Fairy tale", so I drew a rabbit which stood for the rabbit of "Alice in Wonderland" to be the protagonist on the cover. The white circle on the back cover meant the tail of the rabbit, and it was a metaphor of the end at the sametime.

2016新鮮人寶典

Concept

The guidebook was designed for the freshman of National Cheng Kung University. I put the "Banyan tree" on the cover, which could be seen usually in the campus, to stand for NCKU. And I use the main color "pink" and "tiffany green" for creating a dream atmosphere to fit the theme, "Fairy Tale". The main image of the back cover was the deconstruction of the " 成功", which means success in Chinese. It means that you have endless possibilities to create your own success.

Ai教學手冊

Concept

I played a trick on the title, "Ai", as it is not only the abbreviation of "Illustrate", but also the " Artificial Intelligence". As the result, I put a robot head on the cover to create the image of artificial intelligence.
