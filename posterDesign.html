歸設日常

Concept

The image of this poster is that a student of the CPD finally came home to rest at five in the morning and put the " 日常" into the background, suggesting that this is the daily life of the stu dents of the CP D, and r emind them to come to the graduation ceremony, even though they are busy.

設計中

Concept

I linked the design to a continuous cycle of chemical experiments. Only by constantly trying and adjusting can we reconcile the best results.

紙的逆襲

Concept

The tittle of the poster is " 紙的逆襲", means the counterattack of the papper. As the result, I put the broken line of the paper plane on the poster to hint the guests that the mar keting materials co uld become a pa per plane. And the paper plane is one of the counterattack of the paper, isn't it?

NCKU冬展

Concept

The main concept of the poster is "golden ratio". In fact, there are many examples of "golden ratio" in the nature, therefore I created the word " 冬" in Chinese calligraphy style, and made it fit the "golden ratio" at the same time.

Futurismo

Concept

This was my imagination of the Santa Claus in the future, and I used some technology elements, for example the fluorescent on the helmet and the shoes, flickering clothes and the airship, to make it looks futuristic. There was a little story in the poster, the Santa were going to send a Christmas tree to a planet, where was only white trees on it.

沙控

Concept

This poster was design for the beach party which was held by NCKU Industrial Design and Urban Planning. As the result, I combined Chinese word " 沙" with ID, and Chinese word " 控" with UP to be the tittle, and put the shape of " 漁光島"beyond the tittle, where was the place we held the party.
