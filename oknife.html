Oknife&Board

#OKNIFE&BOARD

Product Concept

OKnife&Board are a set of knife and chopping board that design for the elders. Let elders can cut the food more comfortable and easier.

Designer

賴廣穎

呂銘倫

Problem Definition

It is more difficult for elders to cut the food, because of their slow moving and less strength. And when you cut the food with the normal knife, it will hurt your wrist and the muscle of the hand in the long term, let alone the elder. In addition, when finish the cutting, you have to move the food into the wash basket to be the food. To the elder, it usually cost them lots of time when they move the food with the knife or hand. Therefore, we decided to design a set of knife and chopping board to make them engage in these things easier, safer and more comfortable.

Design Concept

OKnife&Board are a set of knife and chopping board that design for the elder. The knife and chopping board were designed through collecting datas of the human body and times of experiments. In this way we had made them used more comfortable and energy efficient. As the result, elder can cook easier or have more will to cook , and their family can feel more relaxed when the elders are cutting the food.

Knife Introduction

According to the research, the tool with 19 degrees of the handle, can makes you use it more comfortable. Because, when you hold the handle in this way, there will be less pressure on your wrist, therefore you feel more comfortable.

We made the top of the handle more flat and wide, so users can put their forefingers on the handle more comfortable and easier to control the knife at the same time.

We designed the knife with knobs on both of the different sides of the handle, so you can put your thumb on it to control the knife easier and hold it more comfortable.

Chopping Board Introduction

To let the elders move the food into the wash basket from the chopping broad faster and more convenient, we added the handles on the board. Therefore, they can move the food without the knife or hand, instead, they can pour the food directly after cutting them.

The big handles make sure that the elders can hold it tightly, prevent from the hands shaking.

Pull up the handles to pour the food quickly and safely.
