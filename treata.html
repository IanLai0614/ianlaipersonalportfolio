Product Concept

TreaTa provides an innovative treatment for ADHD children by creating a massage interaction between parents and children, which promotes positive communication and relationship.

Problem Definition

In Taiwan, about 6% of children suffer from ADHD, which can lead to a negative influence on children, such as bad learning quality or miserable interpersonal relationship. As to associated therapies, long-term analyses suggest that behavioral treatment provides additional benefits beyond medication alone. However, behavioral treatment always puts much burden on both parents and children due to its long treating time. As a result, we aim at designing a product which can help the family with ADHD children get through this hardship during treatment.

Design Concept

We designed "TreaTa" as an entertaining treatment aid, by combining daily interaction with behavioral therapies. We hope parents and children do not consider it’s a treatment but an activity that not only entertains themselves but also brings their relationship closer. While designing the product, the interaction between them is the most important thing.

We relaxes parents by children pounding their back, and give them a reason to praise their kids; in the meantime, we turn the treatment into a musical game for children, they can train their auditory attention while having a better interaction with their parents.

TreaTa Introduction

TreaTa is designed to be simple, cute and loved by children. We use the color contrast between red and blue to create a competitive game atmosphere.

With the laser printing technology, we printed some simple cute elements on the vest to improve the quality and make it lively.

The spring in the button increases the tactile feedback while hit ting it.

A sandwich cloth is used on the back surface to allow the user t o wear without stifling heat and prevent the massage element from directly contacting the wearer, thereby providing a safety protection.

The strap is accompanied by an adjustable buckle that allows any body type of people to wear it .
